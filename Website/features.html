Platform Features

RoadPulse Lite provides essential road safety tools for commuters and administrators through a simple, user-friendly interface.

Main Features

🚨 Incident Reporting

Users can report accidents, roadblocks, heavy traffic, flooding, construction work, and other road incidents.

🛣 Road Status

View current road conditions including traffic flow, blocked roads, and safe travel routes.

📢 Public Alerts

Receive verified alerts published by administrators to keep commuters informed.

🔍 Road Search

Search roads by name and check their latest traffic conditions.

👤 User Accounts

Register and manage user profiles within the RoadPulse Lite system.

🛡 Admin Verification

Administrators review reports before publishing alerts to improve reliability.

How RoadPulse Lite Works

Step 1

A commuter observes a road incident.

Step 2

The commuter reports the incident through the system.

Step 3

An administrator reviews and verifies the report.

Step 4

A verified public alert is shared with other users.

User Roles
